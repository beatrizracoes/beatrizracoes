Project Graphic Identity | Communication Materials
Date May 2023
DescriptionAcademic Project – Graphic identity for the reprise of the documentary series ‘Ways of Seeing’ - the most influential thinkers of our time - at Cinema Ideal. Visual language created with the aim of being provocative, whose graphic elements manage to connect advertising and people, creating a certain nostalgia for what advertising is and how it is much more than an illusion.
