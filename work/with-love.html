Project Graphic Identity | Editorial Design | Web Design
Date April 2024
DescriptionAcademic Project — “With love,” is an emotionally engaging experience that promotes awareness and dialogue on social and ethical issues, such as the importance of love in contemporary society. The project explores the intersection of technology and human emotion, considering how it can both challenge and enhance our ability for empathy. These AI-generated letters, sent from the future, seek to make people reflect and create a sensory and emotionally impactful experience.
